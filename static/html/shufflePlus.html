Are you sure you want to leave?


Revoking access to your Google account and deleting your user account is irreversible!

You can always create a new account by signing in again.



If you still have an active Shuffle+ subscription, your user account will not be deleted,

but you will be logged out and the extension will no longer have access to your Google account.


Signing in again will restore your account and subscription.
